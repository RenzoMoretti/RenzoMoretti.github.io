My motivation

have always spent a lot of time on my computer, often coming up with ideas for things I would like to create or explore. Whenever something sparked my curiosity, I would look into how it was made, and I quickly discovered how fascinating technology can be. That curiosity and excitement for learning new things have stayed with me, and they continue to drive me to develop my skills and understanding.

Why ICT?

I’m not yet completely sure which specific direction I want to take, and that’s exactly what I find so appealing about ICT — it’s such a broad field. It offers countless opportunities to apply my skills in different areas, whether that’s connected to my sport or in a completely different environment, such as a hospital. This flexibility means I can combine my studies with my other passions and adapt my career path as I grow.
