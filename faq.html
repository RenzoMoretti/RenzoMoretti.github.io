Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You can print from your HZ laptop by selecting the printer called HZ Printer. After sending your print job, go to any HZ printer and log in using your HZ pass or your HZ username and password. Choose Print Release to see your jobs and print them. Make sure you have enough credit. For color or single-sided printing, adjust the settings in Printer Properties before sending the job.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

You can scan a document at HZ by logging in at any printer using your HZ pass or your HZ username and password. Select the Scan option from the menu, place your document on the feeder or glass plate, and press Start. If you want to scan double-sided, go to Change settings and enable 2-sided mode. After scanning, log out from the printer. Note: sending scans directly to your personal email is not possible.

How can you book a project space?

You can book a project space at HZ by going to the Self Service Portal. Click on Reserveren, choose the right category, and find the item you want to reserve. Select the date and drag with your mouse to choose the time period. Click Next, check the conditions, fill in the required details, and confirm your reservation.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You cannot park directly at the HZ location on Het Groene Woud, except for one designated spot for people with disabilities or chronic conditions. Students, staff, and visitors can park in the Kousteensedijk parking garage, which is about an 8-minute walk from HZ. After parking, you can go to the JRCZ service desk with your entry ticket to receive an exit ticket.
